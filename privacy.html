Privacy Policy – ConciseAI

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

ConciseAI is a Chrome extension that provides AI-powered summarization of web page content. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines what data we access and how we handle it.

What We Access

  • We access the content of the web page you're currently viewing, only to summarize it.
  • If you enter an API key for Gemini AI, it is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's extension storage.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect, log, or transmit any personal data.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not track your browsing activity or history.

Third-Party Services

We send the article content to the Gemini API to receive AI-generated summaries. This is the only external request made, and it's done securely.

Security

Your Gemini API key is stored locally and never transmitted to any server other than Gemini.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated. We recommend checking this page occasionally for changes.
